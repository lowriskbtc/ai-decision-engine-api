## ğŸ¯ Engagement Tactics

### Reddit Success Tips:
1. **Read subreddit rules first** - Don't get banned
2. **Engage authentically** - Answer every comment
3. **Don't be salesy** - Focus on value and feedback
4. **Post at optimal times** - 9-11 AM or 1-3 PM EST
5. **Use proper formatting** - Make it easy to read

### HackerNews Success Tips:
1. **Post Tuesday-Thursday** - Best traffic days
2. **Be ready to respond** - Answer comments quickly
3. **Don't ask for upvotes** - Against rules
4. **Be technical** - HN audience appreciates depth
5. **Monitor closely** - First hour is critical

### Twitter Growth Tips:
1. **Post consistently** - 3-5 times per day
2. **Engage with others** - Comment on relevant posts
3. **Use threads** - Better engagement than single tweets
4. **Share metrics** - Build in public works
5. **Tag strategically** - @Railway, @stripe (they might retweet)
